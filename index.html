Shayan Hosseini

My name is Shayan Hosseini, natively written as شایان حسینی.
I am currently an undergraduate student pursuing a Bachelor's in Computer Engineering at the ECE Department of University of Tehran.

I have an extensive background in algorithms, combinatorics, and graph theory. When I was in high school, I won a silver medal in the Iranian National Olympiad in Informatics. I achieved 2nd place in Tehran ACM ICPC Regional Contest twice in 2016, 2017. Also, I got 56th place in ACM ICPC World Finals 2017.

From 2015 to 2019 I was working at Cafebazaar, the most popular Android application store in Iran with more than 35 million users. I was a Technical Lead and Product Manager at the infrastructure team building a distributed object storage and a content delivery network. I am one the co-founders of Cafebazaar Cloud, which is a subsidiary of Cafebazaar that provides public cloud services to the other companies.

My main research interest is distributed systems. In the summer of 2019, I was a research intern at Max Planck Institute for Informatic working on fault-tolerant routing problem under the supervision of Prof. Christoph Lenzen.
